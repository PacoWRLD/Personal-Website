A retrospective on Harp Seals

Behind their adorable aesthetic, the harp seal shows many characteristics of intelligence. Harp seals are quick learners, have good coordination, and often show curiosity.

Harp Seal Daily lives

Harp Seals are usually seen dive hunting for fish. Also when it's time to migrate, they usually migrate in herds of up to 10 seals, in which those herds usually make up one giant pack that migrate in unison.

Physical Features

Harp Seals are born snow white, however their appearences can changed drastically throughtout their life. One way is when they shed and grow new patches of fur, meaning they're times when you could see a harp seal with patches surronding its body.
